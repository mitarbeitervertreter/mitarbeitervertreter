Why we are running

For many years, works council elections at our site were conducted as a personal vote. In the upcoming election, employees will vote for the first time using electoral lists.

We see this as an opportunity to bring new ideas, more transparency and a stronger focus on the interests of the workforce.

Our goal is a works council that acts with courage, legal competence and openness towards employees. Decisions that affect the workforce should be communicated clearly and discussed openly.

Employee representation works best when it is close to the workforce, transparent in its actions and independent in its decisions.
